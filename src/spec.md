# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Enhance the Aurelie Gold & Diamonds jewelry e-commerce platform with product category management (22K, 18K, 9K Gold), admin panel capabilities, homepage carousel, Google Pay UPI checkout, and design styling updates.

**Planned changes:**
- Add three product categories: 22K Gold, 18K Gold, and 9K Gold with filtering capabilities
- Add running banner below header displaying "Lab Grown Diamonds" on all pages
- Enhance admin panel with full product management (add/edit/delete), inventory management, order viewing, and carousel management with URL configuration
- Integrate Google Pay UPI for checkout and payment processing
- Create homepage carousel displaying admin-uploaded images with navigation controls
- Update header, footer, and background color styling to match reference site (https://aurelie-fine-jewellery-wgl.caffeine.xyz/admin)

**User-visible outcome:** Users can browse jewelry products by gold categories (22K, 18K, 9K), see a promotional banner for lab grown diamonds, navigate through homepage carousel images, and complete purchases using Google Pay UPI. Administrators can manage products, inventory, orders, and carousel content through an enhanced admin panel.
